Mobile App Development

The use of mobile apps has exploded in recent years offering functionality not available in desktop computers or laptops. This has paved the way for a revolution in the tech industry and I believe the potential of apps will only continue to grow as new functionality is added and our reliance on smart phones increases. I develop my apps using a web based approach allowing myself to code once and release to iOS and Android users.
